Overview

Purpose

Retro video games are one of my favorite pastimes, especially Sony's PlayStation (TM) 2. With one of the largest libraries of classic video games, it is great fun revisiting classic titles. However, it shouldn't take earning a degree on analogue TV signals or spending an arm or leg on a fancy up-scaling device. My aim is to help anyone who has wanted to enjoy their classic games with the hardware they already own, while also learning what options are available to restore or enhance the experience. To that end, my website would offer a simple to understand layout with pages describing ways to connect to your TV and simple modifications so easy your grandma could do it. I already help many friends and people on internet forums. Now is my chance to make a bigger impact on the community.

Audience

My audience are retro gamers and people who are interested in playing Playstation 1 and 2 consoles. The same principles apply to retro consoles as a whole, so most 5th generation console owners and older can also benefit from this site as well.

Content

Home page

This page will include a short introduction to the website, what it contains and how to navigate. A brief start to the connection tutorial is also available to get visitors started right off the bat.

Images for the Home page

Connect a TV

This page will contain details of how to determine what kind of display the visitor has, and based on that make an informed decision about what the best connectors or adapters fit their needs. This page will include helpful photos to help aid in discovering the available connection types.

Images for the Page 2

Picture Quality

This page is all about optimizing the picture quality for your chosen method of connection. From how to pick better cables for signal integrity, to choosing the best settings on your tv to produce the most pleasing image. Pictures and diagrams will be provided for reference, with two options: 1) Original Experience 2) Enhanced Quality. Links to other sites with more detailed explanations will also be provided.
